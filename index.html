About Me

Hi, I'm Alik Pramanick, currently pursuing PhD (January 2021) in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, India, under the joint supervision of Dr. Arijit Sur, and Dr. V. Vijaya Saradhi.

It's been more than 2 years, I'm working in the field of Computer vision and deep learning. I have been implemented a few exciting applications using CV and DL (like Human activity detection from videos, E-Vahannigrani: Detection, identification, and monitoring of vehicles in residential societies, Face mask detection from images, etc.)

My research interests include Image Restoration, Semantic Segmentation, Object Detection, and Underwater Vision.

Currently, looking for some interesting vision problem for my thesis.
